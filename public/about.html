About Bitcoin Pets

Bitcoin Pets is a peer-to-peer marketplace for buying and rehoming domestic animals using Bitcoin. We believe that pet adoption should be simple, transparent, and free from unnecessary intermediaries.

Our Mission

The existing pet marketplace landscape is cluttered with platforms that take large commissions, require fiat payment processors, and hold funds in escrow for days. We set out to build something different: a lightweight marketplace where buyers and sellers transact directly, and where Bitcoin — a borderless, permissionless currency — is the native payment method.

Every listing on Bitcoin Pets is created by a real person with a real pet. Payments go straight from buyer to seller the moment a transaction is broadcast. No middlemen, no chargebacks, no platform fees.

What We Stand For

⚡

Peer-to-Peer

Buyer and seller deal directly. No funds held by a platform, no approval gates.

💰

Zero Commission

Listing and buying are free. The only fees are standard Bitcoin network fees paid to miners.

🐾

Animal Welfare

We prohibit listings for illegal or endangered species. Responsible rehoming is a priority.

Why Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is the most widely understood and trusted cryptocurrency. It settles across borders without requiring either party to have a bank account, a credit card, or permission from a financial institution. For a peer-to-peer marketplace, it is a natural fit.

We display prices in BTC. Sellers set their own asking price, and buyers send payment directly to the seller's wallet address. That's it.

Who Runs This?

Bitcoin Pets is an independent project built and maintained by a small team of Bitcoin enthusiasts and animal lovers. We are not affiliated with any pet industry lobby, financial institution, or cryptocurrency exchange.
