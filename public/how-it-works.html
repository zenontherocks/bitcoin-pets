How Bitcoin Pets Works

A peer-to-peer marketplace for pet adoption and rehoming, powered by Bitcoin. No middlemen, no payment processors — just buyers and sellers transacting directly.

1

Create a free account

Sign up with your email and a username. Add your Bitcoin address in your account settings so buyers know where to send payment when you list a pet.

2

Browse or list a pet

Buying? Browse the full catalogue, filter by species, and read each listing's details — breed, age, gender, health notes, and photos.

Selling? Click Sell a Pet, fill in the details, upload up to 5 photos, and set your price in Bitcoin. Your listing goes live immediately.

3

Contact the seller

When you find a pet you love, reach out to the seller directly. Ask questions, arrange a meet-and-greet, and confirm the pet is a good fit for your home before any money changes hands.

4

Pay with Bitcoin

Once you and the seller agree on terms, send the exact Bitcoin amount to the seller's address shown on the listing. Bitcoin transactions are irreversible, so verify everything first:

  • Double-check the wallet address
  • Confirm the amount in BTC
  • Account for network (miner) fees
  • Wait for sufficient block confirmations
5

Complete the adoption

Once payment is confirmed, coordinate pickup or delivery with the seller. Collect any health records, vaccination certificates, or registration papers that come with the pet.

Common Questions

Does Bitcoin Pets hold my payment?
No. All payments go directly from buyer to seller via Bitcoin. We never hold or touch your funds.
What if a listing turns out to be fraudulent?
Exercise the same caution you would with any peer-to-peer transaction. Meet in person when possible, ask for proof-of-ownership, and never send Bitcoin before you are fully satisfied. Bitcoin transactions cannot be reversed.
Is there a listing fee?
Listing a pet is completely free. We charge no commissions or transaction fees. The only fees you pay are standard Bitcoin network (miner) fees when sending a transaction.
What pets can be listed?
Dogs, cats, birds, fish, reptiles, small animals, and other domestic pets. Listings for exotic, endangered, or illegally kept animals are prohibited and will be removed.
How are prices set?
Sellers set their own price in Bitcoin. Prices reflect the market value of the animal, breeding history, vet care, and whatever the seller deems fair.
